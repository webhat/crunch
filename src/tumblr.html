Why you're not getting the results you want

1. Your approach is too superficial

You try to solve your problems at the surface and not at the source.

When your wife rejects your idea for a romantic weekend in Paris, it feels much safer to leave the plan altogether or try to talk her into it, than to find out what the underlying issue is. If you're more afraid of learning something about yourself and your relationship tha n having your marriage fail, it is indeed the safest option.
