In our coaching relationship however, your willingness to have a honest look at yourself is a lot more important than who I am or what I can do. I am only a facilitator.

To keep that clear I will coach you for a period of no longer than three months. And before we even get started, I want to know that you have a goal that is truly important to you.

Going after that goal creates all kinds of challenges around you and inside you, and that gives us something to talk about.

I combine several approaches to coaching and our coaching can and will take different levels. In short, our conversations may be:

Mindblowing

Unearthing, acknowledging and replacing the unnoticed assumptions that you have made about the world that you live in.

Engaging

Improving your ability to engage yourself and others in your vision, and to listen to theirs.

Practical

Mastering the practical art of developing new habits.

Confronting

Having an honest look at your results and asking feedback from important people in your life.
